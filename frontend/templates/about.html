About Ballard Grappling Club

High-level grappling instruction, strong community, and a training environment designed for long-term growth.

Our Values

Ballard Grappling Club is a student-focused environment that believes that hard work, consistency, and fun are the keys to success. We are devoted to high-quality instruction and building community. Our culture is centered around helping you succeed. Whether, you are interested in competition, self-defense, fitness or simply learning something new, we're here to support you in making progress towards your goals.

Our Space

We're located on Leary Way right on the edge of Ballard and Fremont. Right off the Burke-Gilman trail and with ample parking, we're easily accessible by foot, bike, bus or car. We share our space FUELhouse gym, who share our commitment to building community through fitness and movement.

Meet the Instructor

Ballard Grappling Club is led by Vong Ly. He has been training jiu jitsu since 2011, where he originally began as a hobbyist. He fell in love with the art, problem solving, and community that the sport provided. He has been teaching for 10 years and his proudest moments in the sport have been watching his students succeed. Vong is a one-stripe black belt and has competed regionally. In his free time, he enjoys eating pastries with his wife.
